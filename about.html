About Me

Hi! I’m Ogeday, a Creative Photographer and Videographer based in Bonn with over 5 years of experience in photography and videography, graphic & web design, marketing, and social media management.

I work with passionate artists, brands, and individuals to make them look professional, and stand out above competitors through outstanding creative photography and videography. Together, we can develop the perfect imagery for the press, for your website, and for social media growth!

You’ll always see me with my camera, but it's not the whole story. I'm studying a MSc in Marketing Management. Also, I fed my knowledge further through many courses such as Social Media Management, Filmmaking, Creative Photography, Web Design, Graphic Design and Editorial Production.

“If you want to launch a new business, improve your online presence, or need professional photography for any of your projects, please don’t hesitate to get in touch. The way you tell your story can make all the difference!”

I love learning new skills within the creative industry. This allows me to cover most of the visual aspects of any business.

When I moved to Bonn, I decided to start my own business as a freelance photographer and creative director.

Now, I love helping artists, brands and individuals to create amazing photography so they can look professional and stand out above the crowd!

If you need any help with any visual aspect of your business or yourself as an individual, I’m available worldwide. Please don’t hesitate to contact me through the button below. I would be more than happy to hear your needs and see how I can help you with them!
